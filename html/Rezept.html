Rezept:)

        250g Butter, soft
        250g Sugar
        1 Pinch of Salt
        3 Eggs
        1 Lemon
        500g flour
        1 Eggyellow
        1 TL Milk

Step 1:

 Add Butter, Sugar & Salt and mix it good.

Step 2:

 Add Eggs & Lemon Zest and mix it good.

Step 3:

 Add flour and mix it good. After that pack it like this
 and put it in the fridge for 2h.

Step 4:

 Roll the dough out like this and cut your shapes.
 Preheat your oven at 200°C. Bevor baking put the mix of
 the eggyellow and milk on the cookies

Step 5:

 Bake your cookies at 180°C for about 10min. After that
 they should look like this
